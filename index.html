Our Story

Founded in March 2025, Ukubona LLC is a dynamic startup dedicated to advancing biomedical, clinical, and public health research. We secured our first major client, Johns Hopkins Enterprise, in June 2026, marking a significant milestone in our journey. With expertise in Stata programming, NIH-funded study support, and decision-support application development, we are committed to delivering tailored solutions that drive impactful outcomes. Starting in the DMV region, we aim to scale our services to national and global institutions, transforming research through precision and innovation.
